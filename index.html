Yuheng JIA （贾育衡）

Hi! I will join the Southeast University as an associate professor soon. I am currently a Postdoctoral Fellow of computer science at the City University of Hong Kong. My research interests broadly include topics in machine learning and data representation, such as low-rank tensor/matrix approximation and factorization, unsupervised/semi-supervised clustering, and Bayesian modeling. I defended my thesis on Nov. 2018 and officially received the Ph.D. degree on Feb. 2019 from the Computer Science Department at the City University of Hong Kong, where I was advised by Professor Sam Kwong.
